who am i

Fayez Lahoud

PhD student, Computer Science

I am currently a PhD student in the IVRL group of EPFL, headed by Professor Sabine Süsstrunk. I obtained my Masters degree in Computer Science at EPFL doing my thesis in this same lab.

I have previously worked at Sony Europe Ltd, where I researched the use of convolutional neural network for speech recognition. I have also worked as an intern at Vidinoti SA where I developed a javascript AR tracking library.

My research interests are in Computer Vision and Graphics, specifically to apply machine learning for image processing tasks.
